Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We do not want it, we do not see it, and we do not store it.

1. Local Processing

Ultimate PDF operates entirely within your browser environment. When you edit, convert, or merge a file, the file is processed by the extension's code running on your local machine. It is never uploaded to any external server.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (Name, Email, Address).
  • Document content or metadata.
  • Usage analytics or tracking cookies.

3. Permissions

The extension requires permissions (like "Read/Modify Downloads") solely to save your edited files back to your computer.
